What are We?

We are a Government Minecraft Server aimed to provide an escape from the Hypixel Clones and Earth Servers The limit being your imagination, we provide a platform for you to shine. Be a police officer and stop illegal activities, run for president or be a druglord yourself. The choice is yours.

Why choose us?

Most Servers have crossplay but unlike us there has not been a lot of work on it. Such as texture packs. In the early stages of development we made sure not to add anything which bedrock cannot experience. If java can see and use guns, so should bedrock. This has made it so updates take a while but they release for both versions. We also have been making such servers for years now so we know what people like and what they dont.
